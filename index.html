Welcome

HoeToa is an online resource dedicated to the art and sport of paddling. HoeToa’s mission is to help paddlers discover their competitive edge by sharing insights, tips and training programmes from international paddling champions.



How it works

HoeToa releases crafted training programmes, downloadable books, videos and interviews (with top athletes) once or twice a month - you can receive automatic updates by following our emails (you can of course unsubscribe at any time).
